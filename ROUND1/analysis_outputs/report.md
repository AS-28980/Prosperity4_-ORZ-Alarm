## Key interpretation guide

- `spread = best_ask - best_bid`.
- `bid_depth` and `ask_depth` are total visible volume across top 3 levels.
- `depth_imbalance = (bid_depth - ask_depth) / (bid_depth + ask_depth)`.
- `trade_minus_mid > 0` means trade happened above observed mid.
- For fixed-value products, compare trades/order levels to known fair value.
- For moving products, normalize prices by `mid_price` or your own fair estimate.
